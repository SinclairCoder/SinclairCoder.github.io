## 解决方法
### 1.管理员身份打开cmd窗口
### 2.使用命令 netstat -ano  查看当前端口信息
![8080端口被占用](https://img-blog.csdnimg.cn/20190714211605497.png?x-oss-process=image/watermark,type_ZmFuZ3poZW5naGVpdGk,shadow_10,text_aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L1NpbmNsYWlyV2FuZw==,size_16,color_FFFFFF,t_70)

![杀掉占用8080端口的进程](https://img-blog.csdnimg.cn/20190714211932668.png?x-oss-process=image/watermark,type_ZmFuZ3poZW5naGVpdGk,shadow_10,text_aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L1NpbmNsYWlyV2FuZw==,size_16,color_FFFFFF,t_70)
